Our website is called “GreenHouse” because it helps foster passion projects for those who need help just like how Greenhouses foster plants. Simply, our code allows those with innovative and  creative ideas to get their projects off the ground by asking for assistance and knowledge from mentors and peers. Students can share their interests and expertise through the “Proof of Passion” system to allow for them to showcase past projects. Our app is different from other competitors because Greenhouse is focused and allows innovators to find like minded individuals who want to work with them. Our platform also allows users to connect with people their age group and in their community to easily collaborate. When someone has an idea, but doesn’t have the materials or the expertise to begin designing or creating, it is easy to get help and actually create instead of letting the idea wither.
